About the Yagam

The APTHA Vajapeya Yagam is a sacred 15-day Vedic ritual organized with immense devotion in Palangi village. The ritual was performed under the spiritual guidance of Sri jandhyala Srinivasa Subramanya Sharma.

Each day featured special homams, vedic chants, and worship dedicated to Lord Shiva, promoting peace and spiritual awakening.
